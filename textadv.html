Welcome to the property located on the Haunted Highway...



Sitting on this property is the home of Babylon the Worst. It was said that on the 13th Blood Moon a screaching cry came from the woods. Some say it was a the call of a banshee. By the time the cops arrived Babylon and his wife were murdered. Some say it was by the ghost of his deceased mother. Other's say it was his corrupt buisness partner who was having an affair with him and was worried that the Mrs. might find out...



Step inside their home and encounter the demons that may be lurking in search of the truth..
